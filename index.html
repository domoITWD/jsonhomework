signs of the zodiac

why are they important?

Zodiac signs are based on the belief that people have strong pre-determined personalities. People are born with certain traits and while they change over time, deep down a person will always have the same core personality. The study of personality types can help in your social and professional life by allowing you to better understand yourself as well as how you react to different situations, how others perceive you, and how to work with people of other personality types.
